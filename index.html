بَرَكَاتٌ

Eid Blessings

✦

To my beloved family, dear friends, and every soul I hold close —
near or far, wherever life has taken you in this wide world 🌍

On this most joyful day of Eid, I sit with my hands raised and my heart full, making a quiet, sincere du'a just for you.

May Allah fill your life with so much goodness that your heart cannot contain it all.
May He protect you the way only He can — with mercy that never runs out, and love that never fades. 💙

🤲
Du'a for You

May Allah grant you health so strong that you wake every morning feeling alive, grateful, and at peace.

💛
For Your Heart

May He remove every worry from your chest and replace it with certainty, hope, and a smile that stays.

🌙
For Your Future

May every door that matters open for you, and may your path always be lit by His guidance and grace.

🕌
For Your Faith

May your connection with Allah grow deeper with each day, and may Salah always be your comfort and refuge.

🌹
For Your Family

May Allah bless your home with laughter, harmony, and barakah that spills over into every corner of your life.

⭐
For Your Goals

May every honest effort you make be rewarded, and may Allah make easy what seems impossible.

أَدْعِيَة

Sacred Du'as

✦
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي أَهْلِنَا وَأَصْدِقَائِنَا
Allāhumma bārik lanā fī ahlina wa asdiqā'ina
"O Allah, bless us in our families and our friends"
رَبِّ اجْعَلْنِي مُقِيمَ الصَّلَاةِ وَمِن ذُرِّيَّتِي
"My Lord, make me and my descendants steadfast in prayer"
— Surah Ibrahim 14:40
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً
"Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter"
— Surah Al-Baqarah 2:201
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ
Taqabbalallāhu minnā wa minkum
"May Allah accept from us and from you"
— Traditional Eid Greeting
ﷺ

"Whoever Allah wishes good for, He gives them understanding of the religion and He inspires them with right guidance."

— Prophet Muhammad ﷺ · Bukhari & Muslim

رِسَالَة

A Personal Message

✦

No matter how far apart we are,
know that you are in my heart and in my prayers — always.

Eid is a reminder that we are still connected —
through the same moon, the same faith,
and a love that distance cannot break. 🌙

May this Eid bring you the kind of happiness
that makes your eyes well up with gratitude.

Sent with love & prayers by
Mohammad Saad Raza
